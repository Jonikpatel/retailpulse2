# Executive Summary

This report summarizes key findings from the RetailPulse synthetic e-commerce dataset.

- Electronics drives a disproportionately high share of total revenue relative to its transaction volume, reflecting a high average order value.
- Q4 consistently shows a strong seasonal uplift in revenue, with December representing a large share of annual sales.
- A small cohort of high-value customers contributes a large percentage of total revenue, supporting targeted retention and loyalty strategies.
- Regional performance varies: some regions excel in revenue per transaction, while others rely more on higher order volumes.
- There is evidence of customer churn among early-period buyers, highlighting the need for onboarding and reactivation campaigns.

These insights are generated from synthetic data and should be treated as illustrative rather than prescriptive.
